## 3. Relational Mapping & Cardinality Rules

1. User to Agency (Strict 1:1)
   - Parent Table: User
   - Child Table: Agency
   - Connecting Foreign Key: Agency.userId → User.id
   - Operational Behavior: onDelete: Cascade

2. Agency to Vehicle (One-to-Many, 1:N)
   - Parent Table: Agency
   - Child Table: Vehicle
   - Connecting Foreign Key: Vehicle.agencyId → Agency.id
   - Operational Behavior: onDelete: Cascade

3. Agency to Booking (One-to-Many, 1:N)
   - Parent Table: Agency
   - Child Table: Booking
   - Connecting Foreign Key: Booking.agencyId → Agency.id
   - Operational Behavior: Standard (No Cascade)

4. Vehicle to Booking (One-to-Many, 1:N)
   - Parent Table: Vehicle
   - Child Table: Booking
   - Connecting Foreign Key: Booking.vehicleId → Vehicle.id
   - Operational Behavior: Standard (No Cascade)

5. Customer to Booking (One-to-Many, 1:N)
   - Parent Table: Customer
   - Child Table: Booking
   - Connecting Foreign Key: Booking.customerId → Customer.id
   - Operational Behavior: Standard (No Cascade)
